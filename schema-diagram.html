Implementation notes
Tenant scoping — Collections with tenantId reference Employer (not Tenant). Tenant-scoped: locations, teams, team_groups, users, rosters, daily_shifts, pay_runs, employee_tax_infos.

Employee payroll sub-collections — EmployeeTaxInfo, EmployeeBankDetails, EmployeeContract, EmployeeQualification, and EmployeeCompliance are standalone collections linked via employeeId. The Employee document keeps shortcut refs (taxInfoId, bankDetailsId, contractId).

Computed fields — LocationRoleEnablement.isActive and EmployeeRoleAssignment.isActive are computed from date ranges in pre-save hooks.

Embedded sub-schemas — Schedule (on employees.schedules[]) and Shift (on rosters.shifts[]) are not top-level collections.

Unix timestamps — Tenant, User, and OAuthToken store createdAt/updatedAt as Unix seconds (not Mongoose timestamps).

RuleTemplate / PayrollMapping — These schema modules exist (rule-template.ts, payroll-mapping.ts) but are not re-exported from the schemas barrel (schemas/index.ts) in all environments; they may be imported directly where needed.
